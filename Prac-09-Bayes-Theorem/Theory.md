Theory – Bayes Theorem

Bayes Theorem is used to find conditional probability.

Formula:
P(A|B) = (P(B|A) * P(A)) / P(B)

Terms:
• Prior Probability → Initial probability
• Posterior Probability → Updated probability
• Conditional Probability → P(A|B)
• Joint Probability → P(A ∩ B)

Application:
• Weather prediction
• Medical diagnosis
• Spam filtering

Example:
P(cloudy) = 0.40  
P(rain) = 0.20  
P(cloudy | rain) = 0.85  

P(rain | cloudy) = 0.425
