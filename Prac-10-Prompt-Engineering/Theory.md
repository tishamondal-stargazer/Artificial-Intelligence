Prompt Engineering is the process of designing effective inputs (prompts) to get accurate and relevant outputs from AI models. The quality of output depends heavily on how clearly and specifically the prompt is written.

Key techniques include:

Using clear instructions
Providing context
Defining role (e.g., “You are an AI professor”)
Breaking tasks into steps

Better prompts lead to more structured, accurate, and useful responses. It is widely used in applications like chatbots, content generation, and AI assistants.
